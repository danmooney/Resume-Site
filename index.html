Who am I?

Hi, my name's Sean Mooney, I'm 22 years old, and have been programming since I was 11 years old. I started off by teaching myself to make my own video games with "Klik-n-Play", "GameMaker", and "Multimedia Fusion". Learning to make my own games made me curious to expand my knowledge into other forms of app creation. Since then I've created my own: Websites, Webapps, iOS Apps, and Android Apps, as well as more advanced video games. My work is a relfection of me, personally and professionally, so I take pride in my creations.

After graduating high school, I went on to Norwalk Community College to study Computer science. In the fall of 2015, I graduated from NCC with my Associates in Computer Science. Since then I've gone on to complete Independent Software's A100 program and work within an Agile team to develop a working prototype for Independent Software called "Project Xavier".

Currently, I am doing freelance work building websites for clients ranging from entrepreneurs to home builders. Click the button below to see my whole portfolio.
